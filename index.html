--- layout: single author_profile: false header: image: /assets/images/Ocean_Butterfly_banner.jpeg --- This is the web platform for the US CLIVAR working group (WG) whose aim is to develop strategies, and identify best practices, for improving the understanding, derivation, communication, and utilization of the uncertainties of ocean in-situ, remote, and modeled products related to physical variables

Mission Statement

Objective 1: Develop a community-driven web platform for UQ knowledge and strategies. Objective 2: Produce peer-reviewed open-access articles on UQ for observational and model ocean data. Objective 3: Organize a summer school and/or workshop on UQ.
